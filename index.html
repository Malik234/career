Aircraft Mechanic/Aircraft Maintenance

Job Requirements

Career Overview

Aircraft Mechanic, or an Aircraft Maintenance Technician, perform tasks on repairing vehicles that have the ability to fly in the air. This job can be obtained through going to a trade school, bypassing the need for a four year college for a Bachelor's degree. As an Aircraft Mechanic, you are expected to carry out tasks like inspecting an airplane to make sure it runs safely and smothly, and if not, then you are to identify any issues and solve them as best as possible.
